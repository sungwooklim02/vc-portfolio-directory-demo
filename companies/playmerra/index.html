Co-op puzzle games for families across devices.

Playmerra builds co-op puzzle games for families across devices. Founded in 2024 and based in New York, USA, the team has stayed deliberately close to products people invite into their daily routines.

The product started as an internal tool and grew through word of mouth among early customers. Today Playmerra serves teams across North America, and the roadmap stays intentionally narrow: do a small number of jobs exceptionally well.
